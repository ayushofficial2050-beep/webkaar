How fast is your Internet?

Use the WebKaar Internet Speed Test to verify your WiFi or cellular connection speed. We measure your download speed, ping (latency), and jitter to tell you how stable your connection is.

Understanding Results

  • Ping: Lower is better. Essential for gaming and video calls.
  • Download: Higher is better. Affects web browsing and streaming.
  • Jitter: Lower is better. High jitter means unstable connection.

About Tool

Method: Downloads a test file to measure real-time speed.

Note: Upload speed requires backend servers, so this tool focuses on Download & Stability.

Data Usage: Uses approx 5MB-10MB of data per test.
