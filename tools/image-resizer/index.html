Resize Images Online (Pixel Perfect)

WebKaar Image Resizer is the fastest way to change the dimensions (width and height) of your photos online. Whether you need to resize a photo for Instagram, create a thumbnail for YouTube, or adjust an image for a government application, this tool gives you exact control over pixels.

Key Features

  • Aspect Ratio Lock: Prevents your image from looking stretched or squashed by automatically calculating the height when you enter the width.
  • No Uploads: Your photos are resized directly in your browser for 100% privacy.
  • Support for All Formats: Works perfectly with JPG, PNG, and JPEG files.
