About Age Calculator

The WebKaar Age Calculator is a free online utility that helps you determine your exact age in years, months, and days. While most people know their age in years, calculating the precise number of months and days since you were born can be tricky due to leap years and varying month lengths. Our tool does the complex math for you instantly.

How to Calculate Your Age?

  1. Enter Date of Birth: Select the day, month, and year you were born from the calendar picker.
  2. Select Target Date: By default, it is set to "Today", but you can choose a past or future date to calculate your age at that specific time.
  3. Click Calculate: Hit the button to see your detailed age report instantly.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Job Applications: Many government exams and job applications require your age as of a specific cut-off date (e.g., "Age as of 1st Jan 2026"). This tool gives you that exact figure.
  • Birthday Countdown: Find out exactly how many days are left until your next birthday so you can start planning your party!
  • Fun Stats: Discover interesting facts like the day of the week you were born (e.g., Monday, Friday) and the total number of days you have lived on Earth.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this age calculator accurate?

Yes, our algorithm accounts for leap years (years with 366 days) and the specific number of days in each month (28, 30, or 31) to provide 100% accurate results.

Can I calculate age for a future date?

Absolutely! Just change the "Age at Date" field to any future date to see how old you will be at that time. This is useful for planning retirement or milestones.

Is my data saved?

No. WebKaar operates entirely in your browser. The dates you enter are processed locally on your device and are never sent to any server, ensuring complete privacy.
