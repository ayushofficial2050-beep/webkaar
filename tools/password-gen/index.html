Secure Random Password Generator

In today's digital world, using "123456" or "password" is a huge risk. The WebKaar Password Generator helps you create strong, unhackable passwords instantly. Protect your Facebook, Instagram, Google, and Bank accounts with military-grade random passwords.

Why is this tool safe?

  • 100% Client-Side: The password is generated inside your phone/computer using JavaScript.
  • No Server Storage: We do not send or save your passwords anywhere. It disappears when you refresh.
  • Customizable: Choose length, symbols, and numbers to meet specific site requirements.

Tips for a Strong Password

A strong password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols (!@#$). Avoid using your name or birthdate.

How to use?

1. Set Length: Use the slider to choose how long you want your password to be.

2. Select Options: Check boxes to include Capital letters, Numbers, or Symbols for better security.

3. Generate: Click the blue button to create a unique password.

4. Copy: Click the copy icon to save it to your clipboard.
