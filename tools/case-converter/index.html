WebKaar Case Converter is a handy tool designed to adjust the capitalization of your text documents. Whether you accidentally left Caps Lock on or want to format a title properly, we've got you covered.

Available Modes

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capitals.
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters.
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence.
