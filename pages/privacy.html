Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to WebKaar. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by WebKaar and how we use it.

Core Promise: We do not store your files. Tools like Image Compressor, Converter, etc., process your files locally in your browser.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent) to help us improve user experience.
  • Device Information: Basic info like device type (Mobile/Desktop) and browser version to optimize our layout.

We do not collect Personal Identifiable Information (PII) like your name, phone number, or address unless you explicitly contact us.

2. Security & File Processing

Most of our tools (Image Compression, PDF Tools, Converters) are built using Client-Side Technology.

  • Your files never leave your device.
  • Processing happens inside your web browser.
  • We do not have access to view or copy your uploaded files.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use "Local Storage" to save your preferences, such as:

  • Theme Preference: To remember if you prefer Dark Mode or Light Mode.
  • Recent Tools: To show you tools you use frequently.

You can clear this data anytime by clearing your browser cache.

4. Third-Party Links

WebKaar may contain links to other websites (e.g., YouTube, Instagram). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us, and we advise you to review their Privacy Policy.
